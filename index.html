Biography

I'm 27 year old IT technician, currently working for Stanley Black & Decker. Most of my career I spent in support role in which I feel I am a strong link for a team. Confidence in my skills created a comfort zone in a present job, what I feel is not really great for personal growth. Thats why I decided to shift my interest into something that might potentially be a career-changing experience.

Almost a year to the date, I started a Kodilla Web-Developer Bootcamp. For the past year I had a chance to develop new skills that enabled to get a potential new profession and try new things. So far my coding experience wasn't easy but hopefully with enough practice and determination I will be able to land a developer job and fulfill my dream of being able to work and travel simultaneously.

I am fast-paced, socially adaptable hardworker with a jam for sports and everything games related. I believe that the most important thing for everybody is to work as hard as you can to fulfill your goals and constantly develop.
